Functions

to_charlist(atom) 

to_charlist(atom()) :: charlist()

Converts an atom to a charlist.

Inlined by the compiler.

Examples 

iex> Atom.to_charlist(:"An atom")
'An atom'

to_string(atom) 

to_string(atom()) :: String.t()

Converts an atom to a string.

Inlined by the compiler.

Examples 

iex> Atom.to_string(:foo)
"foo"
